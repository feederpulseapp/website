Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

FeederPulse respects your privacy.

Data Collection

FeederPulse does not collect, store, or share personal information.

The application operates primarily offline and stores fishing session data locally on your device only.

Permissions

The app may use the following device features:

  • Notifications: used for fishing timer alerts and reminders.
  • Vibration: used for timer and event feedback.

These features are used only for core app functionality.

Third-Party Services

FeederPulse uses third-party services to track usage statistics and optimize performance:

  • Google Analytics: We collect anonymous, aggregated website traffic statistics to help us improve your landing page experience.

Advertising: While we may introduce advertising in the future to help support the development of FeederPulse, there are currently no advertisements active on either the website or the mobile app. No tracking identifiers or data are collected or shared for advertising purposes at this time.

Data Storage

All session data, counters, and timer settings are stored locally on your device.

No cloud synchronization is currently implemented.

Children's Privacy

FeederPulse does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
